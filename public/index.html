  • Kp (Proportional): Increases response speed but can cause overshoot
  • Ki (Integral): Eliminates steady-state error but can cause instability
  • Kd (Derivative): Reduces overshoot and improves stability, but sensitive to noise

  • Test Different Inputs: Step, ramp, and sine tests reveal different characteristics
